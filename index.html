    CGPC

    An app to manage activities of CGPC (career guidence and placement cell)

    Students can login to view upcomming interviews and register the same from the app. They can also get information about previous interviews like no of students appeared, no of students placed, questions asked, average LPA,etc

    Placement cell volunteers can use the app to update the details of inrterviews and inform the students
